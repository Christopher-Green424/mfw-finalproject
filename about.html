Great cooking channels:

These are some fun and enlightening YouTube channels about cooking.

Binging with Babish

This channel has a lot of solid fundamental cooking advice, but there is a lot of advanced cooking as well. Though be warned, his videos often assume you have a lot of kitchen and fridge/freezer space. Oh, and he does a series of videos about cooking in movies, shows, and cartoons!

Pro Home Cooks

This channel has a lot of good techniques and recipes too, though I haven't seen as many of his videos as I have of Babish's. However, unlike Babish, his cooking is a lot less "refined". Low-cost food, late-night munchies recipes, and basic condiment/sauce recipes can be readily found here.

Townsends

This channel is not just dedicated to cooking, though it is the majority of their content. Very relaxing and informative videos, like if Bob Ross were a historical reenactor. There's some real oddball recipes from back in the 18th century though, and he makes them... though he's a positive kind of person and will find something to compliment about what he's eating, even if it's a plain unsalted cracker.
